Lasagna

Delicious lasagna. A family favorite!

  • noodles
  • margarine
  • flour
  • salt
  • pepper
  • milk
  • parmesan cheese
  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Drain.
  2. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  3. Melt margarine in a skillet over low heat; cook and stir flour, salt, and pepper into the melted margarine until smooth and bubbling, 2 to 3 minutes.

Macaroni and cheese

A very quick and easy fix to a tasty side-dish.

  • macaroni
  • butter
  • flour
  • salt
  1. Cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, 8 minutes. Drain.
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat; stir in flour, salt, and pepper until smooth, about 5 minutes.
  3. Fold macaroni into cheese sauce until coated.
