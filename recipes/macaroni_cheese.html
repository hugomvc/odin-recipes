Macaroni and cheese

A very quick and easy fix to a tasty side-dish.

  • macaroni
  • butter
  • flour
  • salt
  1. Cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, 8 minutes. Drain.
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat; stir in flour, salt, and pepper until smooth, about 5 minutes.
  3. Fold macaroni into cheese sauce until coated.
